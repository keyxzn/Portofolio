Comparative Analysis of YOLOv8, YOLOv12, and Faster R-CNN for Motorcycle Helmet Detection

Artificial intelligence-based helmet detection systems have developed rapidly with deep learning technology. Object detection models such as YOLOv8, YOLOv12, and Faster R-CNN have proven effective in identifying objects in digital imagery. YOLOv12 is the latest version of the YOLO family, offering further improvements in speed and accuracy for real-time object detection. However, YOLOv8 remains the most commonly used model for object identification due to its established reliability and widespread adoption. Meanwhile, Faster R-CNN has the advantage of performing more precise detection through a region-based network (RPN) approach to proposals.
